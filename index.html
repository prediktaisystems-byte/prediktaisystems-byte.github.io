Frequently Asked Questions
What are Hidden Markets? +
Hidden markets are statistical betting options most people don't know about. Unlike traditional markets like Home Win or Over 1.5 Goals, hidden markets analyze specific in-game events like Yellow Cards, No Brace, or Ball Not Hitting Post. This gives you an edge because fewer people analyze them, meaning better odds and higher win rates.
What are Familiar Markets? +
Familiar markets are the common betting options most people use, such as Match Winner (1X2), Over/Under 2.5 Goals, and Both Teams To Score (BTTS). These markets are popular because they are easy to understand but are also highly competitive and often less predictable over time.
